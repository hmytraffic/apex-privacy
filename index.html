2. What do we collect and why?

We collect information to provide you with the Services, personalize your coaching experience, operate and secure our platform, and improve the user experience.

2.1 Categories of personal data, purposes, and lawful basis (GDPR)

1. Account & profile data. Name or display name, email address, password hash, age/date of birth or age confirmation, optional profile photo, optional gender.
Purpose: Create and manage your account; provide the Services; communicate with you about service messages, technical updates, and support.
Lawful basis: Contract; Legitimate interests (support, security).

2. Fitness profile data. Goals, training preferences, experience level, schedule, optional injury/limitations you choose to provide.
Purpose: Personalize training plans and coaching guidance; adapt recommendations.
Lawful basis: Contract; and where required, Explicit consent (if treated as sensitive data).

3. Workout & performance data. Workouts, distance, duration, pace, cadence, splits, session timestamps, notes, perceived effort, and similar training metrics.
Purpose: Provide training history, progress tracking, insights, and personalized coaching.
Lawful basis: Contract.

4. Health-related signals (optional). For example: heart rate, recovery indicators, injury notes — if you provide or import them.
Purpose: Enable richer insights and personalization.
Lawful basis: Explicit consent where required for sensitive data; otherwise Contract.

5. Location / GPS data (optional). If you record outdoor workouts and enable permissions.
Purpose: Route mapping, distance/pace calculation, workout records.
Lawful basis: Consent (device permission); sometimes Contract (feature you request).

6. Connected services data (optional). Data from partners you connect (e.g., Strava, Garmin, Apple Health, Google Fit, COROS).
Purpose: Import/export workouts; consolidate your training history; improve personalization.
Lawful basis: Consent (connection authorization); Contract.

7. Technical & usage data. IP address, device type, OS/app version, language, approximate location from IP, server logs, crash reports, feature usage.
Purpose: Operate, secure, and troubleshoot the Services; prevent fraud/abuse; improve performance.
Lawful basis: Legitimate interests (security, improvement); sometimes Consent (where required for tracking).

8. Support communications. In-app messages where applicable, emails, bug reports.
Purpose: Provide customer support and respond to your requests.
Lawful basis: Contract; Legitimate interests (support quality).

9. Marketing preferences (optional). Newsletter opt-in/out and campaign engagement.
Purpose: Send updates, offers, and news (where permitted).
Lawful basis: Consent (marketing).

10. Subscription data. App Store / Google Play subscription status, receipt validation data, plan entitlements.
Purpose: Manage access to paid features and subscription status.
Lawful basis: Contract; Legal obligation (accounting where applicable).

Important note on sensitive data. Some fitness and health-related information can be considered "special category" (sensitive) personal data under GDPR depending on context. Where required, we rely on explicit consent and provide in-app controls.

2.2 AI features and processing

CHAMA provides AI-powered coaching features. These features may process information you provide (such as your goals, training preferences, and workout summaries) to generate personalized recommendations.

AI Partner. We may use Anthropic as an AI processing partner to generate certain coaching outputs.

When AI features are enabled, we may share limited data with Anthropic that is reasonably necessary to generate the requested output (for example: plan structure, session summaries, distance/pace trends, and your stated goals). We aim to minimize the data shared and avoid sending direct identifiers where feasible.

Anthropic processes data for the purpose of providing the AI functionality to CHAMA. The handling of such data is governed by our contractual arrangements with Anthropic and applicable law.

Please do not include highly sensitive personal information (e.g., medical diagnoses, full address, or other sensitive details) in free-text fields unless strictly necessary, as free text may be used to generate AI outputs.

2.3 Cookies / SDKs / tracking (minimal)

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking SDKs for targeted advertising.

We may process basic technical data (such as IP address, device information, and server logs) to operate, secure, and improve reliability of the Services.

Where required by law, we will request consent before using non-essential cookies or similar technologies.

2.4 Other scenarios

Corporate transactions. If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, reorganization, financing, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, personal data may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to appropriate safeguards.

Aggregated statistics. We may use aggregated or de-identified data to understand trends and improve the Services. Such data is not intended to identify you.

We do not sell your personal data.

AI Partner (Anthropic). If you use AI coaching features, we may send limited data needed to generate coaching outputs to Anthropic, under contract and for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

Legal and compliance. We may disclose personal data where required to comply with law, enforce our Terms, or protect rights, property, or safety.

7. What are my rights?

Under GDPR (and similar laws), you may have rights to request:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure of your data in certain circumstances
  • Restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Data portability (where applicable)

To exercise any of these rights, contact support@hellochama.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In France, this is the CNIL.
