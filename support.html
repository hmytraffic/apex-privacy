The App

4 screens. Everything you need.

Today

Your day starts here.

See your daily briefing, physio metrics from your wearable, today's session with full exercise details, and personalized nutrition — all in one place. Kai adapts everything based on your condition, your recovery, and the weather.

Coach

Talk to Kai. He gets it.

Ask anything about training, nutrition, recovery, or supplements. Kai gives you straight, science-backed answers. Need to adjust your plan, swap an exercise, or add a session? Just ask — Kai handles it in one message.

Plan

Your program, your rhythm.

View your complete training plan by phase, week, or month. Every session is structured with warm-up, main work, and cooldown. See what's coming, track what's done, and watch your periodization unfold across Build, Peak, and Taper phases.

Performance

Follow the process. See the progress.

Track your consistency, physical condition, and recovery level at a glance. Weekly reports break down your sessions, duration, load, heart rate zones, and tonnage. Daily and monthly reports give you the full picture of your training journey.

Session

Every exercise, explained.

Open any session to see the full breakdown: warm-up, main work, cooldown. Each exercise comes with sets, reps, RPE, rest times, and coaching cues. Kai even briefs you with a 45-second audio before you start.

End of Session

Every rep counts.

When your session is done, see your full stats: duration, strain, calories, heart rate, tonnage, and heart rate zones. Share your results on Instagram or debrief with Kai for a detailed AI analysis.

Plan Overview

See the big picture.

Your complete program at a glance: race day countdown, overall progress, phases with Build, Peak, and Taper. Know exactly where you are and what's coming next in your training journey.
